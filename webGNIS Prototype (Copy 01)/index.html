Welcome to Web GNIS

At NAMRIA, the Geodetic Network Information System (GNIS) is a specialized database designed to support geodetic and mapping activities in the Philippines. The GNIS contains detailed descriptions of each Geodetic Control Point (GCP), including its geographic location and precise geographic coordinates such as latitude, longitude, and elevation. GNIS maintains extensive records for each GCP, including geographic coordinates such as latitude, longitude, and elevation.

By providing reliable geographic coordinates, GNIS ensures that all mapping and surveying activities in the Philippines are based on consistent and accurate data. This is essential for various applications including land development, infrastructure planning, and environmental management.

The system is designed to be user-friendly, allowing easy access to GCP information. This ensures that surveyors and mappers can quickly find the data they need to carry out their work efficiently.
